Coal Mines of the Anthracite Coal Region

In northeastern Pennsylvania, several counties make up the Anthracite Coal Region, a region which is dominated by its presence of anthracite coal and its coal mines.

Those counties are:

  1. Northumberland, Columbia, Schuylkill, Carbon, Luzerne, Lackawanna

These counties were dominated by mainly German and Eastern European immigrants from coal companies going overseas to hire cheap labor."
